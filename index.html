About

I am a Masters in Robotics student at Georgia Tech.My research interests are in Neural Perception, especially at the intersection of Computer Graphics and Vision. I have done research on generative models for image manipulation, 3D reconstruction from monocular in-the-wild images and worked on autonomous systems for aerial vehicles. I obtained my undergraduate degree in Mechanical Engineering from IIT Dharwad.
